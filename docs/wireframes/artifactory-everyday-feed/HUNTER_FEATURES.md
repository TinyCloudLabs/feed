# Hunter feature proposals

Source: Hunter/Sam/Roman product discussion, 2026-07-10.

This list keeps concrete product proposals from Hunter and folds repeated or
overlapping examples into a single feature. Transcript insights and chat-history
insights are the existing Feed baseline, so they are not counted as new pages.

## Nearer-term explorations

1. **Feed switcher** — Move between focused feeds such as personal insights,
   curated discovery, work, health, or a group. Each feed behaves like a small
   app with its own sources and preferences.
2. **Curated discovery** — Find useful outside articles from the interests Feed
   learns through private context and explicit reactions. The content is
   selected by AI, not generated by AI, and preserves the original source.
3. **Shared group feed** — A shared space where members contribute Feed items
   and see a group-specific ranking shaped by collective activity.
4. **Share an item to a group** — Copy a useful artifact from a personal Feed
   into a chosen group Feed without exposing the private source conversations
   behind it.
5. **Invite and manage people** — Create a group Feed, invite members, and make
   the allowed actions understandable without exposing delegation mechanics.
6. **Group learning and sharing rules** — Let reactions and shared items tune a
   group Feed; optionally define narrow rules for content that may be suggested
   or automatically shared later.

## Future-horizon exploration

7. **Budgeted physical outputs** — Give a Feed workflow a spending limit and
   ask it to turn a period of life into a physical artifact, such as a photo
   book or printed monthly recap. Purchasing remains review-gated.

## Optional advanced control

8. **Human-readable agent configuration** — Show the routines that make the
   Feed, their cadence, sources, expected cost, owner, and status. Let a user
   edit, pause, remove, or add a routine without reading cron, soul files,
   manifests, or workflow code. Paid/private routines disclose owner, access,
   and price even when their implementation stays hidden.

## Deliberate grouping

- Hunter's work/chat/health/sleep/calendar examples are variants of the Feed
  switcher, not separate features.
- Virality, RSS-like syndication, and group memes are outcomes or content types
  of shared feeds, not standalone pages.
- Automatic sharing is shown inside group learning because Hunter described it
  as a later extension of group feeds.
- Allocating money to an agent and producing a photo book are one future
  budgeted-output concept.
- The workflow library, recurring jobs, cadence controls, and agent mechanics
  are grouped into one human-readable Feed routine view.
